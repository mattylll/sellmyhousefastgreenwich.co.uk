How It Works

Our simple 4-step process makes selling your house fast and stress-free.

Step 1

Get Your Free Valuation

Fill in our simple online form or give us a call. We will ask a few basic questions about your property to get started. There is no obligation and no pressure.

Step 2

Receive Your Cash Offer

Our team will assess your property and present you with a fair, no-obligation cash offer within 24 hours. We are completely transparent about how we arrive at our figure.

Step 3

Choose Your Completion Date

If you are happy with our offer, you choose when to complete. We can work to your timeline, whether that is 7 days or several weeks. You are in control.

Step 4

Get Paid

On completion day, the cash is transferred directly to your solicitor. No estate agent fees, no hidden costs. The amount we agreed is the amount you receive.
